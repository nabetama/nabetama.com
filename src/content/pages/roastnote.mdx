## RoastNote

RoastNote is a home coffee roasting log app for iOS.

Record your roast sessions with bean info, timing, crack events, weight changes, and more to build your personal roasting history.

### Features

- **Roast Timer** - Track elapsed time and record first crack / second crack timing
- **Roast Log** - Save bean name, origin, weight, notes, and flavor tags
- **DTR Setting** - Set your target Development Time Ratio
- **Local Storage** - All data is stored on your device for privacy

## Privacy Policy for RoastNote

**Effective date:** February 21, 2026

RoastNote is a coffee roasting log app developed by Mao Nabeta.

This Privacy Policy explains what data RoastNote uses, how it is used, and how in-app purchases are handled.

## Summary

- RoastNote stores your roast records locally on your device.
- RoastNote does not operate a developer-run cloud backend for syncing your roast data.
- RoastNote does not include third-party analytics or ad SDKs.
- Subscription purchases are processed by Apple through StoreKit.

## Data Stored by the App

RoastNote stores the following data locally on your device:

- Bean information (for example: name, origin, variety, process)
- Roast session records (for example: time, crack events, weights, notes, flavor tags)
- App settings (for example: DTR target and related preferences)

This local storage is used only to provide app features (recording, viewing, and managing your roast logs).

## Data Collection and Sharing

RoastNote does **not** sell your personal data.

RoastNote does **not** send your roast logs to a developer-operated server.

RoastNote does **not** use third-party advertising or behavior-tracking SDKs.

## Subscriptions and Payments

RoastNote offers an optional subscription.

- Purchases, renewals, billing, and receipt handling are performed by Apple.
- RoastNote uses Apple StoreKit APIs to read subscription product information and entitlement status.
- Payment information is not processed or stored by the developer.

For Apple’s handling of payment-related data, please refer to Apple’s privacy policy.

## External Links

RoastNote may open Apple-managed pages, such as the subscription management page in the App Store.

## Data Retention and Deletion

Because roast data is stored locally, data remains on your device until you delete it in the app or uninstall the app.

## Children’s Privacy

RoastNote is not directed to children under 13, and no intentional collection of children’s personal data is performed by the developer.

## Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page with a revised effective date.
